Get going in 30 seconds

  1. Download and open the DMG, drag switchdial to Applications.
  2. Open it. macOS will ask for Accessibility permission — that's how it sees the Option+click gesture. Grant it in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility.
  3. On first launch, Settings opens automatically. Click the empty dial slots to add your six most-used apps.
  4. Anywhere, anytime: hold ⌥, click, flick toward the app. Release.
